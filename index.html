Bee and PuppyCat is an animated series on Cartoon Hangover, created by Natasha Allegri and produced by Frederator Studios. The show first aired on July 11, 2013. It follows the lives of Bee, a directionless twenty-something woman, and PuppyCat, a mysterious creature that literally falls into her life one day. Together they work intergalactic temp jobs to pay the rent!
