Characters

PuppyCat

PuppyCat came into Bee's life under strange circumstances. When she took him in, and they became friends, co-workers, and roommates, and have been rarely seen apart from each other. He doesn't consider Bee to be his owner and that he is not a pet. He believes that he is more like a business associate or friend of Bee's and not belonging to her as a normal cat or dog would. Is he a dog or a cat? Or both? Nobody knows.

PuppyCat
(To Deckard) "Pick up my groceries, peasant!"

Bee

Bee is someone who likes to space out sometimes, and impulsive. She often acts without thinking which leads to her getting into trouble. She is very kind and didn't hesitate to bring PuppyCat home when she first found him. She doesn't give up easily; even though she has no skills or magic of her own, she does what she can to win fights and help her friends.

Bee
(To Deckard) "Too bad I can't make a career out of getting fired, right? I'd be like, a CEO or a manager. Which one's more impressive? ...President?"

Deckard

Deckard is a friend of Bee who lives in the same apartment complex as Bee. Deckard is a quiet individual who seems passive when compared to the people around him. He also tends to put others' needs before his own. He has a talent in cooking and works as a prep cook. He even got accepted into a fancy culinary school.

Deckard
(To Bee) "That's okay. We can work with this." (He says this while panicking)
